Experience

Geshe Michael Roach

One of the greatest scholars and meditation teachers of modern times

As an entrepreneur who helped build the fastest growing New York company using the knowledge and wisdom from ancient writings — one of the company's key success factors was its regular meditation retreats.

Michael Roach studied the ancient wisdom for more than 25 years and became the first westerner in 600 years to graduate as a Geshe — the highest degree of a Buddhist scholar.

With this knowledge, he helped build the most successful company in New York history. With annual sales of $250 million, it became the world's leading diamond jewelry company — in 2009 it was sold to Warren Buffett.

He has written countless books including various bestsellers such as The Diamond Cutter, explaining how he achieved this success through the application of these wisdoms. After selling the company, he went on a long silent retreat of more than 3 years.

Learn from this master of meditation who, like no other, is able to present authentic ancient knowledge in a way we can understand and easily integrate into our everyday lives.
